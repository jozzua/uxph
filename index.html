Speakers

  • Whitney Hess

    Whitney Hess coaches business leaders on how to more mindfully and compassionately design their products, their companies and themselves. She writes at Pleasure & Pain and co-hosts the podcast Designing Yourself. Her life’s mission is to put humanity back into business.

  • Patti Hunt

    Patti is a founder and director of On-off Design & Technology. She has previously held senior positions as User Experience (UX) consultant, Service Designer and Strategic Design Director for companies in Australia and Hong Kong.

  • Kristin Low

    Kristin Low is a founder of On-off Design & Technology. He has a background in user experience (UX) design, digital service design and product management. Kristin is an active member of the design and business communities in Hong Kong as an MC, mentor and trainer as well as hosting jams and hackathons.
